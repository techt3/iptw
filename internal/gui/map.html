About the Game ▼

IP Travel Wallpaper turns every website visit into a world adventure. Each outbound connection you make is a "visit" to that country — explore the globe just by browsing the web.

🌍
Virtual Travel — connections to foreign IPs count as visits to that country.
🏳️
1–9 visits — the country shows its national flag: a fresh destination worth exploring.
😴
10+ visits — the country goes "boring." Keep visiting and you'll trigger the Matrix Prison effect.
🎯
Target Countries — a random unvisited country is highlighted. Race to make it boring and earn the Fastest Traveler achievement!
  • Visit every country on the map
  • Complete entire continents
  • Discover local news & authentic voices
  • Escape the US / EU / China hosting bubble
  • Find websites in microstates & island nations
